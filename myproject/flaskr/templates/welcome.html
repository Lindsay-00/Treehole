Hi, welcome.

This is an open forum without memories. Anything you post or respond to here will be automatically deleted after three days.

We hope you will feel safe to speak your mind here - those thoughts and ambiguities you somehow can’t really share with friends and family. We hope you find here a place of comfort and a source of support. This means we will need YOU to help create that environment for everyone. So, we ask you to exercise your common sense when posting/replying, specifically:

Don’t say things you wouldn’t want to hear being said to you.

Don’t post content that you know will get you/anyone else into legal trouble.

If you think you might need professional mental health support, please click here.
